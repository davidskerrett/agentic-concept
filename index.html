AI-Powered Commerce Transformation.

Designed and delivered an AI-driven shopping experience that transformed how customers discovered and purchased products on mobile. Kobalt Music · AWAL

Reimagining the Independent Artist Experience.

Designed and delivered an AI-driven, agentic customer experience that gave independent artists personalised insights into audiences, streaming performance and royalties. By turning complex data into intelligent, actionable guidance, the platform empowered artists to make better decisions at every stage of their careers. The work earned multiple industry awards and helped establish AWAL as a technology leader ahead of its $430 million acquisition by Sony Music.

III
Haribo · Retail+

Reimagining the Confectionery Shopping Moment.

Designed and delivered an AI-driven, agentic retail experience for Haribo that turned passing shoppers into active participants through real-time, personalised gamified promotions. By orchestrating adaptive prize mechanics and dynamic reward logic at the point of decision, the platform transformed a single QR scan into an intelligent, individualised play journey, no two shopper experiences ever quite the same. The work was recognised by Trend Hunter as a leading example of AI-fuelled retail gamification, helping establish Retail+ as the intelligent engine behind one of confectionery retail's most talked-about activations.

IV
JTI Ploom · Travel Retail

Future-Forward Duty-Free Personalisation at Scale.

JTI's Ploom rollout paired an AI-informed, agentic retail layer with bespoke, real-time device customisation, giving travellers a personalised path from discovery to a product built for them, on the spot, at the point of sale. By turning a routine duty-free purchase into an intelligent, individualised moment, the activation, delivered with Dubai Airports, Dubai Duty Free and JCDecaux, transformed personalisation from a marketing promise into a physical, on-counter reality. The pattern has since scaled across Geneva, Zurich, Milan, Rome, Athens, Madrid, Tokyo and Dubai, establishing bespoke, agentic personalisation as the new benchmark for premium travel retail.
